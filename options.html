WHY DO WE NEED WEBCAM DATA?




Senti uses computer vision to predict emotions and analyze how different websites make you feel. Your webcam data is vital in allowing us to determine which sites affect you the most and let us help cater a selection of sites you might find helpful, as well as provide tips and support if necessary. By letting Senti use your webcam, you’re ensuring that we can provide you with the best service possible.



NOTE




THIS APP USES CLARIFAI
Clarifai is an artificial intelligence and computer vision platform – to detect how you’re feeling at each website. We’ve trained the detection algorithm ourselves to achieve the most accurate results.

WE DON'T SAVE YOUR IMAGES
Once data about your image has been returned from computer vision analysis, your photo is deleted.

WANT TO STOP LIVE?
Senti can disabled at any time through the Google Chrome Extensions menu.
